# Prompt Engineering

Prompt engineering is the practice of writing and structuring instructions so a model reliably produces the desired output—despite inherent non-determinism.

---

## Practical guidance

From [[prompting]] (esp. OpenAI docs):\n
- treat prompts as product code: test, version, and evaluate\n
- pin model snapshots to stabilize behavior\n
- use high-authority instruction channels where available (system/developer/instructions)\n
